Support Drug Awareness... Save a Life

Contribute to prevention programs and help protect the youth

At Kobciye Foundation, we believe that raising awareness about drug abuse is essential
in safeguarding the well-being of our youth and protecting communities.
Through education programs, counseling, and prevention campaigns,
we strive to equip individuals with knowledge and support systems
to make safe and healthy life choices.

Our goal is to reduce addiction risks and empower families
with the tools they need to overcome drug-related challenges.

Our drug awareness initiatives include educational workshops,
school outreach programs, counseling support, and community campaigns.
We work closely with parents, teachers, and local leaders
to build a drug-free future for the next generation.
Together, we aim to create safer, healthier communities.
