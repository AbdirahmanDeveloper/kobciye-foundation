Water Relief Mission Brings Hope

Kobciye Foundation has begun drilling a new water borehole to provide clean
and reliable water to communities suffering from chronic water shortages.
The project aims to reduce long-distance water walks, improve hygiene, and
support families, livestock, and local farming. Engineers and community
volunteers are working together to ensure safe, sustainable water access.
Once completed, the borehole will serve hundreds of households daily.
