Support a Relief Mission

Donate and bring hope to families in crisis

At Kobciye Foundation, our relief campaigns aim to respond quickly and effectively
to emergencies affecting vulnerable communities.
We provide food packs, shelter materials, emergency water access, and essential supplies
to families facing disasters such as drought, flooding, displacement, or conflict.
Through these coordinated relief efforts,
we ensure that those in crisis receive life-saving assistance when they need it most.

Our relief initiatives have supported thousands of families by delivering emergency aid,
distributing food and water, and providing temporary shelters.
Working with local communities and volunteers, we ensure rapid and effective response
during the most challenging times.
